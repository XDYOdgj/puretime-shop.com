Moen Aromatherapy Handshower with INLY Technology

Transform your daily shower into a serene and rejuvenating ritual with the Moen Aromatherapy Handshower. This high-tech handheld shower combines the power of aromatherapy with modern bathroom design to bring the feel of a spa into your own home. The patented INLY Technology ensures that essential oils are infused directly into the water, enhancing your shower experience with soothing scents tailored to your mood.

Key Features

  • Patented INLY Technology infuses essential oils directly into water
  • Includes INLY starter pack with four spa-inspired fragrances
  • Customizable aromatherapy experience for different moods
  • Easy-to-use infusion dial for adjusting dilution settings
  • Magnetic dock provides secure and effortless attachment

Why Choose This Product?

Whether you're seeking calm, energy, focus, or relaxation, the Moen Aromatherapy Handshower offers a personalized and multi-sensory escape. The magnetic dock holds your handshower securely in place, making it easy to use while your eyes are closed, ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted experience. This product delivers fresh, scented water without leaving any residue on your skin, hair, or bathtub, making it perfect for those who value both hygiene and wellness.
