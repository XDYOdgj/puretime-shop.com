KOHLER Awaken B Series Handshower

The KOHLER Awaken B-Series Handshower is more than just a functional bathroom accessory—it's a statement of refined style and intelligent design. Inspired by the organic beauty of nature, its polished chrome finish adds a touch of sophistication to your space, making it a perfect complement to modern and contemporary interiors.

With three spray modes—wide coverage for a relaxing soak, intense drenching for a thorough rinse, and a targeted spray for focused cleaning or massage—you can customize your shower experience to suit every need. The thumb tab allows for easy and seamless switching between these modes, ensuring uninterrupted comfort and convenience.

Thoughtfully crafted for everyday use, the handshower features an ergonomic grip and balanced weight, making it effortless to hold and maneuver. This design ensures a comfortable experience even during extended showers. Its adjustable angle allows for precise targeting of specific areas, offering versatility for both personal care and utility tasks.

Additionally, the MasterClean spray face is designed for effortless cleaning, reducing maintenance hassle and keeping your handshower looking like new. Whether you're looking to enhance your daily routine or upgrade your bathroom’s aesthetic, the KOHLER Awaken B-Series Handshower delivers a seamless blend of style, performance, and durability.
