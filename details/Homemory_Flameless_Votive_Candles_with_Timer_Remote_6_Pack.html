Homemory Flameless Votive Candles

Elevate your space with these beautifully crafted flameless votive candles. Meticulously designed to resemble real candles, they feature a lifelike wax construction and a natural 3D flame that flickers and dances, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. Whether you're setting the mood for a romantic dinner, a cozy gathering, or a festive holiday, these candles add an elegant touch without the risks of open flames or messy wax drips.

Designed with convenience in mind, these candles come with a remote control for synchronized lighting across multiple units. Perfect for grand events like weddings or parties, you can easily manage the lighting, ensuring harmony and visual impact. The built-in timer function offers customizable settings of 2, 4, 6, or 8 hours, allowing the candles to turn on and off automatically. Plus, the battery-operated feature ensures long-lasting use with included CR2450 batteries, making them ready for any celebration or decor setup.
