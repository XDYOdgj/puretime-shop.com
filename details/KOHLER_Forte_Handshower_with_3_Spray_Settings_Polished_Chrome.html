Kohler Forte Handshower

Elevate your daily shower routine with the Kohler Forte Handshower. Combining elegance, efficiency, and versatility, this modern fixture is designed to bring comfort and style to any bathroom.

Advanced 3 Spray Modes

Experience a variety of spray options that cater to different moods and needs. From full coverage for thorough rinsing to a pulsating massage for deep relaxation and a silky spray for a refined, spa-like feel, the Forte offers something for everyone.

High Water Pressure & Energy Efficiency

Enjoy a strong and steady stream of water with a 2.5 GPM (9.5 lpm) maximum flow rate. This ensures optimal performance without compromising on energy efficiency, making it a smart choice for eco-conscious homeowners.

Polished Chrome Design

The sleek and modern polished chrome finish of the Forte Handshower adds a touch of sophistication. It complements contemporary bathroom designs and stands out as a premium addition to your home spa.

Easy to Clean with MasterClean Sprayface

Designed for hassle-free maintenance, the MasterClean sprayface prevents mineral buildup and keeps your showerhead looking like new for longer. This makes cleaning more efficient and less time-consuming.

Simple Operation with Thumb Tab

Switch between spray functions with ease using the intuitive thumb tab. This allows seamless transitions and ensures that you can customize your shower experience quickly without disrupting your routine.

Durable Build & Universal Compatibility

Engineered for long-term use, the Forte Handshower features a durable chrome finish that resists tarnishing, helping maintain its appearance over time. It is also compatible with multiple mounting systems, offering flexible installation options.

Whether you're looking to upgrade your bathroom with a stylish and functional showerhead, or simply want to enjoy a more comfortable shower experience, the Kohler Forte Handshower is the perfect choice. It brings the luxury of a spa into your home while being easy to maintain and energy-efficient.
